Ingredients:
 - 4 large eggs
 - 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese (shredded)
 - salt
 - pepper
 

Directions:
 1. Preheat skillet to medium heat. 
 
 2. In a large bowl, mix eggs, salt, and pepper to taste.
 
 3. Add mixture to skillet. Stir constantly while slowly adding cheese.

 4. Cook to desired firmness.
